# Services & Pricing

This page outlines how I typically work with clients and teams, along with common pricing models.  
All engagements are scoped clearly before work begins.

---

## CRM Management Services

### CRM Setup & Implementation
Best for new CRMs or major rebuilds.

**Includes:**
- CRM setup and configuration
- Buyer & seller pipelines
- Lead stages and tagging
- Automation workflows
- Email and SMS campaign setup
- Lead routing and lifecycle logic
- Basic integrations (forms, calendars, websites)

**Pricing model:**
- Fixed price (project-based)
- Scope-dependent

---

### CRM Cleanup & Optimization
For existing CRMs that are messy, underperforming, or not being used.

**Includes:**
- Pipeline and workflow review
- Automation fixes
- Data cleanup and lead mapping
- Campaign optimization
- Performance troubleshooting
- Usability improvements

**Pricing model:**
- Fixed price or hourly (depending on scope)

---

### Automation & Integrations
For businesses that need systems to work together reliably.

**Includes:**
- Zapier integrations
- CRM-to-website connections
- Lead source integrations
- Automation troubleshooting
- End-to-end testing

**Pricing model:**
- Fixed price per integration
- Hourly for complex workflows

## IT Support & Systems Services

### System Setup & Troubleshooting
Focused on reliability, not just setup.

**Includes:**
- Domain and DNS configuration
- Email delivery setup (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Subdomain setup for email systems
- Website form and chatbot troubleshooting
- Calendar and booking system fixes
- CRM and tool connectivity issues

**Pricing model:**
- Hourly or fixed price (issue-based)

---

### Ongoing CRM & IT Support
For teams that want continuous support and system stability.

**Includes:**
- CRM maintenance
- Automation monitoring
- Issue resolution
- Minor updates and improvements
- Technical support as needed

**Pricing model:**
- Monthly retainer
- Hourly support blocks

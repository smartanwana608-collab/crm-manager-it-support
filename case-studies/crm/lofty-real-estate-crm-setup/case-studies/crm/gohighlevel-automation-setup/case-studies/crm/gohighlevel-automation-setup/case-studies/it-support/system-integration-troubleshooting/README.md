## The Problems

Across different environments, the most common issues included:

- Emails going to spam or not delivering
- Missing or incorrect DNS records
- Email subdomains not configured properly
- SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records missing or misaligned
- Automations not triggering as expected
- Leads not syncing between websites and CRMs
- Broken Zapier integrations
- Website forms not submitting or not passing data correctly
- Chatbots failing to capture or route leads
- Incorrect data mapping during imports
- User access and permission issues
- Inconsistent system behavior across tools

These issues caused lost leads, poor communication, manual workarounds, and reduced trust in the systems.

---

## What I Did

My role focused on identifying root causes and restoring reliable system performance:

- Diagnosed technical issues across connected platforms
- Audited DNS records and fixed misconfigurations
- Set up and corrected email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Configured email subdomains for proper delivery
- Rebuilt and tested broken automation workflows
- Reconnected and validated Zapier integrations
- Mapped, cleaned, and re-imported contact data
- Tested end-to-end lead flow from website to CRM
- Fixed form and chatbot routing issues
- Resolved user access and permission problems
- Documented fixes and provided ongoing technical support

## Outcome

After troubleshooting and system optimization:

- Email delivery improved and spam issues were reduced
- Automations ran reliably without manual intervention
- Leads synced correctly across platforms
- Website forms and chatbots captured leads consistently
- System downtime and errors decreased
- Clients regained confidence in their tools
- Support requests became less frequent due to improved stability
